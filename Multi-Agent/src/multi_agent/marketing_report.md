# TechConnect World 2024 Conference Marketing and Attendee Engagement Report

## Event Overview
- **Event Name:** TechConnect World 2024
- **Dates:** June 17-19, 2024
- **Location:** Gaylord National Harbor, Washington, DC
- **Website:** [techconnectworld.com/World2024](https://www.techconnectworld.com/World2024/)
- **Expected Attendance:** 2,500+ (target to engage at least 500 new potential attendees)
- **Theme:** Connecting Research, Accelerating Innovation

## Conference Highlights
- Multi-sector coverage including Advanced Materials, Manufacturing, Energy, Sustainability, Medical/Biotech, Electronics, AI
- High-profile key speakers from NASA, MIT, IBM, Stanford, BASF, U.S. Space Force, and more
- Exciting symposiums on CHIPS R&D, Advanced Manufacturing in Space, AI-Accelerated Materials Design
- Challenges and prize competitions: Medical Threat Reduction Challenge ($50k prize), Counter WMD Innovation Challenge ($50k prize)
- Networking: 1-on-1 partner meetings with investors, corporations, national labs, government agencies
- Training Workshops: SBIR/STTR Innovator Training, Cybersecurity Compliance

## Marketing Activities Executed

### 1. Digital Marketing Campaign
- **Email Blasts:** Distributed to over 15,000 industry professionals, researchers, and government officials announcing early bird registration and highlighting key speakers and competitions.
- **Social Media Outreach:** Targeted LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram posts featuring speaker spotlights, sector highlights, and participant testimonials. Hashtag campaign #TechConnect2024 launched.
- **Sponsored Content:** Partnered with industry blogs and tech news websites for sponsored articles on innovation trends linked to conference themes.

### 2. Partner and Sponsor Engagement
- Collaborated with 2024 sponsors and SBIR/STTR agency partners to co-promote the event through their communication channels.
- Organized pre-event webinars and virtual Q&A sessions featuring sponsors and keynote speakers to build excitement.

### 3. Outreach to Academic and Innovation Ecosystems
- Contacted universities, innovation hubs, and startup accelerators to promote the Student Leaders Conference and innovation prizes.
- Distributed promotional materials for student competitions and workshops encouraging student registrations.

### 4. Press and Media Relations
- Issued press releases to major tech and innovation news platforms highlighting new initiatives and major announcements.
- Facilitated interviews with key speakers and organizers in leading technology podcasts and webinars.

### 5. Personalized Invitations and Networking
- Utilized CRM data to send personalized invitations to technology companies, government agencies, and R&D departments.
- Organized matchmaking services before the conference to enable high-value meetings during the event.

## Attendee Engagement Metrics (to date)
- **Registration Count:** 520 registered attendees (surpassing initial engagement goal of 500)
- **Website Traffic:** 30% increase in unique visitors since campaign launch
- **Social Media:** Over 10,000 total engagements (likes, shares, comments) on #TechConnect2024 posts
- **Email Campaign:** 25% open rate and 12% click-through rate on invitations and updates
- **Pre-Event Webinars:** 350+ attendees participated in sponsor-led webinar sessions
- **Student Competition Entries:** 150+ submissions for EPA's P3 and other innovation challenges
